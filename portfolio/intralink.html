Intralink Group

Development of a custom Content Management System and website for this international provider of market access and business development services to European and North American companies targeting China, Japan, Korea and Taiwan.

About the project

Intralink Group is the market leading provider of market access services for companies looking to break into or develop their business in Asia.

I've worked with them for a number of years, most recently implementing a new website with a refreshed design and a custom Content Management System

The custom CMS was developed from scratch, and I'm currently extending it for use in other projects. The concepts are based on my earlier Braincube CMS, but retargeted for greater flexibility and ease-of-use.

Key features

  • Clean, accessible and cross-platform design, using HTML5 and CSS3.
  • Easy-to-use Content Management system with drag-and-drop file and content management.
  • Multilingual frontend with full Unicode support for Chinese, Korean and Japanese scripts.
  • Full translation support within the Content Management system
  • Comprehensive Search Engine Optimisation
